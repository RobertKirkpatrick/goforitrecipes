title: JDawgs Sauce

ingredients:
- 1 cup ketchup
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 1 tbsp bbq sauce
- ¼ cup honey
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1½ tsp worcestershire sauce
- ½ tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp black pepper

directions:
- Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Heat till bubbling. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
