ingredients:
- 1 can carrots
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp parmesan cheese
- salt & pepper to taste

directions:
- Combine carrots and butter, heat through (yes, you can microwave)
- Mash carrots, adding parmesan when smooshy
- Salt and pepper how you like
